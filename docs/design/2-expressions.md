# Expressions

Various expression types and operators

---

## Arithmetic

* `+`:  add
* `-`:  subtract (binary), negate (unary)
* `*`:  multiply
* `/`:  divide
* `%`:  modulo
* `**`: exponent

---

## Logic

* `!`:  not
* `&&`: and
* `||`: or
* `^^`: xor

---

## Comparison

* `==`: equal
* `!=`: not equal
* `>`:  greater
* `<`:  less
* `>=`: greater or equal
* `<=`: less or equal

---

## Bitwise

* `~`: not
* `&`: and
* `|`: or
* `^`: xor

---

## Other

* `()`: function call
* `$`:  dereference pointer
* `#`:  borrow pointer
